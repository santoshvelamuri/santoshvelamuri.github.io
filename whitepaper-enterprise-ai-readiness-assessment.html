Enterprise AI Readiness Assessment

A structured approach to evaluating your organization's readiness for AI adoption — covering strategy alignment, data maturity, capability gaps, and governance foundations.

Most organizations are eager to adopt AI, but few have a clear understanding of how ready they truly are. This white paper presents a practical assessment framework that helps enterprise leaders evaluate AI readiness across four critical dimensions.

Use this guide to identify gaps, prioritize investments, and build a foundation for scalable AI adoption.

What's Inside

Key topics covered in this white paper:

Strategy Alignment

How to assess whether your AI initiatives are connected to strategic business objectives and capability priorities.

Data Maturity

A framework for evaluating data quality, governance, and architecture readiness for AI workloads.

Capability Gaps

Identifying the people, process, and technology gaps that must be addressed before scaling AI.

Governance Foundations

Establishing the policies, standards, and oversight mechanisms needed for responsible AI deployment.

Who This Is For

This white paper is designed for enterprise architects, technology leaders, and strategy teams who are evaluating or planning AI adoption at scale. Whether you're just starting with AI pilots or looking to move beyond experimentation, this assessment framework provides a structured path forward.
