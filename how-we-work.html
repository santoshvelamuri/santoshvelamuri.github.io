How We Work

Our proven methodology for turning strategy into executable architecture.

Three Phase Approach

1

Discover

Understand your landscape (2 weeks)

We start with a comprehensive discovery to understand your organization, strategy, current architecture, and the gaps that need to be addressed.

  • Stakeholder interviews with executives and technical leaders
  • Existing architecture audit
  • Strategic alignment assessment
  • Capability gap analysis
  • Dependency mapping across teams

Deliverables

  • Discovery Report
  • Current State Architecture
  • Gap Assessment
  • Stakeholder Recommendations
2

Define

Create your target vision

Based on discovery findings, we work with your leadership to define the target architecture and align all stakeholders on the vision and priorities.

  • Target architecture design
  • Business capability model alignment
  • Technology stack recommendations
  • Operating model definition
  • Phased transformation roadmap

Deliverables

  • Target Architecture Document
  • Business Capability Model
  • Transformation Roadmap
  • Operating Model Design
  • Executive Presentation
3

Deliver

Execute with visibility and alignment

We support your execution with ongoing guidance to ensure delivery stays aligned with the target architecture and business outcomes.

  • Execution planning and sequencing
  • Decision-making frameworks
  • Monthly steering committee guidance
  • Architecture review and validation
  • Outcomes tracking and adjustment

Ongoing Support

  • Monthly Review Meetings
  • Architecture Guidance
  • Decision Documentation
  • Outcomes Dashboard

Engagement Model
