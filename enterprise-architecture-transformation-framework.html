4-Layer Advisory Framework

Enterprise clarity is built in layers.

Layer 1 — Structure

Enterprise Capability Blueprint

Purpose: Define how the business is structured.

Focus:

  • Business capability model
  • Strategy alignment
  • Capability ownership
  • Capability maturity
  • Capability-based roadmap

Outcome: A structured, strategy-aligned enterprise foundation.

Layer 2 — Portfolio

Application Portfolio Management Blueprint

Purpose: Align applications to capabilities.

Focus:

  • Application inventory
  • Capability-to-application mapping
  • Portfolio health assessment
  • Rationalization opportunities
  • Modernization roadmap

Outcome: Cost control, reduced duplication, and modernization clarity.

Layer 3 — Governance

Architecture Governance Blueprint

Purpose: Control and guide architectural decisions.

Focus:

  • Decision rights
  • Architecture review model
  • Lifecycle checkpoints
  • Standards enforcement
  • Exception governance

Outcome: Reduced fragmentation and predictable decision-making.

Layer 4 — Innovation

AI Opportunity Blueprint

Purpose: Identify and prioritize innovation investments.

Focus:

  • AI use-case discovery
  • Value vs feasibility scoring
  • Readiness assessment
  • AI roadmap

Outcome: Structured innovation aligned to business capabilities.
