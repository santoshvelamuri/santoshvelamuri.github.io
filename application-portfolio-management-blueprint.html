Application Portfolio Management Blueprint

Gain visibility, reduce redundancy, and align your application landscape to business priorities.

Many organizations operate with fragmented application landscapes, overlapping systems, unclear ownership, rising costs, and growing technical debt.

The Application Portfolio Management Blueprint is a focused 4-week engagement that helps you assess your application landscape, align it to business capabilities, and define a structured roadmap for rationalization and modernization.

Duration

4 Weeks

Engagement Type

Fixed-scope advisory sprint

Typical Participants

CIO / CTO, Enterprise Architects, Application Owners, Portfolio & Transformation Leaders

Deliverables

Application portfolio assessment, rationalization analysis, capability alignment view, modernization roadmap, executive decision pack

The Challenge

Most organisations lack a clear, structured view of their application portfolio.

Common issues include:

  • Overlapping applications serving similar functions
  • High maintenance costs with unclear value
  • Shadow IT and duplicate solutions
  • Aging systems increasing risk
  • Poor visibility into technical debt
  • No structured modernization roadmap

Critical questions we help answer:

  • Which applications truly support strategic capabilities?
  • Where are we duplicating functionality?
  • Which systems should we invest in, modernize, replace, or retire?
  • Where is technical debt creating risk?
  • How do we prioritize rationalization efforts?

Without portfolio discipline, costs rise while agility declines.

What You Walk Away With

At the end of this engagement, you will have:

01

Application Portfolio Inventory & Classification

A structured, validated view of your application landscape.

02

Business Capability Alignment View

Clear mapping of applications to business capabilities.

03

Portfolio Health Assessment

Evaluation of cost, risk, complexity, and technical condition.

05

Application Modernization Roadmap

Where Application Portfolio Management Creates Impact

A structured APM approach drives:

Reduced application redundancy

Lower operational costs

Improved agility

Effective portfolio management shifts IT from reactive maintenance to strategic enablement.
